Privacy Policy

Tally Court

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Tally Court does not collect any personal data. There is no account, no sign-up, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs and no tracking.

What stays on your device

  • Scores, match history and settings are stored only on your device.
  • If you use both the iPhone and Apple Watch app, this data syncs directly between your own devices using Apple's WatchConnectivity. It is never sent to us or to any server.

Apple Health (optional)

If you enable Workout, the app starts a workout session on your Apple Watch and reads your heart rate and active energy during a match. This is shown live and, if you keep the option on, saved to the Apple Health app on your device. This information is processed only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can turn it off any time in Settings.

No servers, no sharing

We operate no servers and receive no data from the app. Because nothing is collected or transmitted, there is nothing for us to share, sell, or delete on your behalf. To remove all local data, simply delete the app.
