Tally Court

Keep score for tennis & badminton - on your Apple Watch and iPhone.

Two big tap zones, a clear score, serve indicator and match history. Tennis and badminton treated equally - configurable sets, tiebreaks, no-ad, points and caps.

  • watchOS-first, with a full iPhone companion using the same engine.
  • Settings and history sync between your iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Optional Apple Health workout with live heart rate.
  • No account, no ads, no tracking - everything stays on your device.
