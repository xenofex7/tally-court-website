Quick tips

  • Score a point: tap the left or right half of the screen.
  • Undo, Reset, End: use the bar at the bottom of the match screen (iPhone) or the menu on the Watch.
  • Delete a history entry: swipe it to the left in History.
  • Sync: settings and match history sync automatically between your iPhone and Apple Watch when they are together.
  • Keep the screen on / heart rate: enable Workout in Settings.
  • Rules: sets, tiebreaks, no-ad (tennis) and points, win-by, cap (badminton) are all configurable in Settings.

Requirements

iPhone with iOS 17 or later, and/or Apple Watch with watchOS 10 or later.
